Privacy Policy

Last updated: June

This Privacy Policy describes how FinalWord ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS word search game.

Information We Collect

FinalWord is designed to store most information locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress, such as your current level
  • App preferences, such as sound and haptics settings
  • In-app purchase status for ad removal

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or contact details through normal use of the app.

Advertising

FinalWord uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob and its partners may collect information such as device identifiers and app usage information to serve and measure ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's partner sites policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple may provide us with purchase confirmation needed to unlock ad-free access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help us operate the app, including Apple and Google AdMob, as described above.

Children's Privacy

FinalWord is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at magnetoryus@gmail.com.
